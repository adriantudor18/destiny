Destiny 2 Gameplay Basics

Game Modes

Story Missions

Progress through the main storyline and explore the game's rich narrative.

Crucible (PvP)

Competitive multiplayer where Guardians battle each other.

Strikes

Cooperative missions where 3 players fight against AI enemies.

Raids

Complex, challenging missions for 6 players requiring teamwork.

Core Mechanics

  • Weapon Types: Kinetic, Energy, and Power weapons
  • Armor and Mods: Customize your Guardian's capabilities
  • Subclasses: Different ability trees for each class
