Destiny 2 Character Classes

Titan

The Tank of Destiny 2. Titans are heavily armored warriors who excel in close combat and provide team support.

  • Strong defensive capabilities
  • Powerful melee attacks
  • Can create protective barriers

Hunter

The agile and precise class. Hunters are masters of mobility and precision attacks.

  • High mobility
  • Precision weapons specialist
  • Excellent for solo play

Warlock

The magic-wielding class. Warlocks combine powerful abilities with ranged combat skills.

  • Powerful ability-based combat
  • Area of effect damage
  • Team support abilities
