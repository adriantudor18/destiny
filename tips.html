Top Beginner Tips for Destiny 2

Getting Started

  1. Complete the New Light tutorial
  2. Experiment with different weapon types
  3. Don't rush through missions
  4. Join a clan for support
  5. Learn your class abilities

Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring your inventory management
  • Underestimating the importance of mods
  • Not communicating in team-based modes
  • Neglecting to upgrade your gear

Pro Tips

Power Level

Focus on increasing your power level gradually

Seasonal Content

Always check current seasonal activities

Practice

Don't get discouraged - everyone starts somewhere!
